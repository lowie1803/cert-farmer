notwithstanding | notwithstanding the risks | despite something, used in formal writing | Notwithstanding the risks, the policy was approved.

similarly | similarly, other studies | to draw a comparison with the previous point | The first experiment failed; similarly, the replication yielded no results.

specifically | specifically, three factors | to introduce a precise detail or subset | The regulation targets one sector; specifically, heavy manufacturing.

in conclusion | in conclusion, this paper | to signal the final summary of an argument | In conclusion, this paper has argued that regulation alone is insufficient.

all things considered | all things considered, the approach | after taking everything into account | All things considered, the hybrid approach appears most viable.
