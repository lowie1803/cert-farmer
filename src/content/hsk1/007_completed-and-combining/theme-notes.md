# Week 7 — Completed Actions & Combining Sentences

The 了 (le) particle is *the* particle of HSK. It has more than one job, but HSK 1 needs only one of them: marking that an action **happened / was completed**.

## Completed-action 了

> **Subject + Verb + 了 + (Object)**

| Sentence | English |
|----------|---------|
| 我吃了饭。 | I ate (the meal). |
| 他喝了三杯茶。 | He drank three cups of tea. |
| 我们看了那本书。 | We read that book. |
| 你去了北京吗？ | Did you go to Beijing? |

A few rules to memorize as a block:

1. **了 follows the verb**, before (or instead of) the object: V-了-O.
2. The action is **completed**, not just past. 了 is **aspect**, not tense. A future-completed sentence can use 了: 明天我吃了饭去看你 = "tomorrow, after I've eaten, I'll go see you."
3. **Negation drops 了** and uses 没(有):
   - ✓ 我吃了饭 → I ate.
   - ✗ 我没吃了饭. (Wrong — never combine 没 with 了.)
   - ✓ 我没吃饭 → I didn't eat.
4. **Yes/no questions** with 了 → tack on 吗:
   - 你去了商店吗？ → Did you go to the store?

## Two-clause sentences

You're ready to glue two clauses together. The HSK 1 connectors are simple:

| Connector | Meaning | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| (no connector, just a comma) | sequencing | 我去商店，买水。 — I'm going to the store to buy water. |
| 和 hé | "and" — joins **nouns**, not clauses | 我和我哥哥去学校。 |
| 也 yě | "also" — sits **before the verb**, not at the start | 我也是学生。 |
| 都 dōu | "all / both" — same slot as 也, before the verb | 他们都是中国人。 |

> ⚠️ **和 is for nouns only in HSK 1.** Wrong: ✘ 我吃饭和我喝水. Right: ✓ 我吃饭，我喝水. — Use a comma to join clauses.

> ⚠️ **也 / 都 sit before the verb.** Wrong: ✘ 也我是学生. Right: ✓ 我也是学生.

### Movement and transport

| Hanzi | Pinyin | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 来 | lái | come |
| 去 | qù | go |
| 回 | huí | return |
| 坐 | zuò | sit / take (transport) |
| 开 | kāi | drive / open |
| 飞机 | fēijī | airplane |
| 火车 | huǒchē | train |
| 出租车 | chūzūchē | taxi |

Note the pattern **坐 + transport**: 坐飞机 (take a plane), 坐火车 (take a train), 坐出租车 (take a taxi). For driving yourself, 开 is the verb: 开车 (drive a car). Walking is just 走路 (zǒu lù).
