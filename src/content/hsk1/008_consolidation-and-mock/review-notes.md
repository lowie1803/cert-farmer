# Week 8 — Final-Week Review

No new vocabulary. The job this week is to **stop leaking** on the four classic HSK 1 traps. If you can avoid them, you will read and score above the level your vocabulary alone would predict.

## Error-killer 1 — 是 vs 很 with adjectives

- **是 + Noun**, never adjective.
- **(很) + Adjective**, never 是.
- 我**是**学生 ✓ — noun predicate
- 我**很**好 ✓ — adjective predicate
- ✘ 我**是**好 — beginner tell

## Error-killer 2 — 不有 → 没有

- 有 takes 没, period. **不有 is never grammatical.**
- 我**没有**钱 ✓ — "I don't have money"
- ✘ 我**不有**钱

(With a completed verb, you also drop 了: 我**没**吃饭, not 我没吃**了**饭.)

## Error-killer 3 — 两 vs 二

- **两** before a measure word (and quasi-MW like 点, 年, 天): 两个人, 两本书, 两点, 两年.
- **二** in numbers, ordinals, math: 二十, 第二, 十二月.
- Common slip: ✘ 二个人 → ✓ 两个人.

## Error-killer 4 — Completed 了 word order & negation

- Affirmative: **V-了-O** (我吃了饭) or end-of-sentence 了 (我吃饭了).
- Negative: drop 了, use 没: 我**没**吃饭. **Never** combine 没 with 了.
- Yes/no question: tack 吗 at the end: 你去**了**北京**吗**？

## Tone-3 sandhi & 不/一 sandhi (quick recap)

- 3-3 → 2-3. 你好 = ní hǎo.
- 不 + tone-4 → bú. 不是 = bú shì.
- 一 + tone-4 → yí. 一个 = yí ge.
- 一 + tones 1/2/3 → yì. 一年 = yì nián.

## Question-word reminder

- Question words **stay in place** — they don't move to the front.
- Don't mix 吗 with a question word. 你吃**什么**？ ✓ — 你吃**什么吗**？ ✘

## How to take the mock exam

- The exam below is timed (35 min, 40 questions). Treat it like a real sitting.
- Aim for **70%** to "pass". Score below that → re-do weak weeks before HSK 2.
- Do **not** look up answers during the timer. Mark guesses; review explanations after.

Good luck. 加油 (jiā yóu)!
