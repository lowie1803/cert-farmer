# Market Hours & Sessions

## Regular Trading Hours (US)

The US stock market is open **Monday through Friday**:

| Session | Time (Eastern) | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Pre-market | 4:00 AM – 9:30 AM | Lower volume, wider spreads |
| **Regular hours** | **9:30 AM – 4:00 PM** | Full liquidity, best prices |
| After-hours | 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM | Lower volume, wider spreads |

The market is **closed** on weekends and US holidays (New Year's, MLK Day, Presidents' Day, Good Friday, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas).

## Pre-Market & After-Hours Trading

Also called **extended hours trading**:

- Available through most brokers
- Uses **limit orders only** (no market orders)
- Lower volume means wider bid-ask spreads
- Prices can be more volatile
- Earnings reports are often released before or after market hours, causing big moves

**When it's useful**: Reacting to earnings announcements or major news outside regular hours.

**Risk**: You may get worse prices due to low liquidity.

## Global Market Hours

Markets around the world operate in different time zones, creating a near-24-hour cycle:

| Region | Key Markets | Hours (Eastern) |
|--------|------------|-----------------|
| Asia | Tokyo, Shanghai, Hong Kong | 7:00 PM – 4:00 AM |
| Europe | London, Frankfurt | 3:00 AM – 11:30 AM |
| Americas | NYSE, NASDAQ | 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM |

Events in Asian or European markets overnight can influence US opening prices.

## Key Dates to Watch

- **Earnings season** — Companies report quarterly results (Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct)
- **FOMC meetings** — Federal Reserve interest rate decisions (8 per year)
- **Options expiration** — Third Friday of each month (increased volatility)
- **Triple witching** — Quarterly expiration of stock options, index options, and futures (Mar, Jun, Sep, Dec)

## Key Takeaway

> Regular hours (9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET) offer the best liquidity. Extended hours exist but come with wider spreads and more risk. Global events overnight can move US markets at the open.
