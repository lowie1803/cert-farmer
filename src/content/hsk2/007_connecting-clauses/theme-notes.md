## Targets

- **因为…所以…** (yīnwèi … suǒyǐ …) — *because … therefore …*. Both halves are usually present in spoken Chinese (unlike English, which usually drops one).
- **但是 / 可是** — *but*. Sits at the start of the second clause: 我想去，但是我没有时间.
- **如果…就…** — *if … then …*. 如果下雨，我就不去.
- **Vocab cluster:** reasoning / connective vocab — 因为, 所以, 但是, 可是, 如果, 还是.
