# Week 6 — Modals II: 会 vs 能 vs 可以

> 🚧 **Authoring in progress.**

## Targets

- **会 (huì)** — *know how* (learned skill). 我会游泳 = *I can swim (I learned).* 
- **能 (néng)** — *capacity / circumstantial ability*. 我今天能来 = *I can come today (nothing's blocking me).*
- **可以 (kěyǐ)** — *permission / it's allowed*. 这里可以吃饭吗？ = *Is eating allowed here?*
- The three overlap. Quick test: skill → 会, "able right now / has the ability" → 能, "permitted" → 可以.
- **Vocab cluster:** situational-ability verbs (carry, open, ride…).
