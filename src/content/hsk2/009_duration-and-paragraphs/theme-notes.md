# Week 9 — Duration & Paragraphs

> 🚧 **Authoring in progress.**

## Targets

- **Duration complement:** *Subject + V + 了 + duration + (Object)*. 我学了两年中文 = *I've been studying Chinese for two years.*
- Distinguish **duration** (how long) from **time-when** (at what time). Time-when sits before the verb; duration sits **after** it.
- **Connected paragraphs:** stringing 2–3 sentences with this week's connectors so a short story holds together.
- **Vocab cluster:** duration & quantity words — 一会儿, 几天, 多长时间, etc.
