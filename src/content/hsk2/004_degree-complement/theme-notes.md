# Week 4 — Degree Complement (得)

> 🚧 **Authoring in progress.**

## Targets

- **Grammar spine:** the **得-complement** — *V + 得 + descriptor*. 他跑得很快 = *He runs fast*. 你说得很好 = *You speak well*.
- **Word-order trap:** the 得-complement attaches to the **verb**, not the noun. If there is a direct object, you may need to repeat the verb: *他说中文说得很好*.
- **Three 'de' particles to keep straight:**
  - 的 — possessive / modifier (week 3 of HSK 1).
  - 得 — degree complement (this week).
  - 地 — adverbial marker (later).
- **Vocab cluster:** manner-adjectives + more verbs.
