## Targets

- **A-不-A pattern**: 你**去不去**？ = *Are you going (or not)?* A neutral yes/no question that replaces 吗 in many contexts. 是不是, 好不好, 想不想.
- **为什么 (wèishénme)** — *why*.
- **怎么 (zěnme)** — *how / how come*.
- **怎么样 (zěnmeyàng)** — *how about it / how is it*. 这个怎么样？ = *How's this one?*
- **Vocab cluster:** weather (天气, 晴, 阴, 雨, 雪), travel/transport extras, shopping & money (买, 卖, 块, 钱).
