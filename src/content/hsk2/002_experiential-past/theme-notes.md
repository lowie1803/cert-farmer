# Week 2 — Experiential Past (过)

## Targets

- **Grammar spine:** the experiential particle **过** (`guo`): *V-过-O* = "have ever V-ed O" (去过北京 — *I've been to Beijing*).
- **Contrast:** **了 vs 过.** 了 reports a *specific* completed event; 过 reports an experience-in-the-past with no specific time.
- **Negation:** 没有 + V + 过 (我没去过北京). The 过 stays.
- **Vocab cluster:** experiential-past / time-reference words — 以前 (yǐqián, "before / in the past"), 以后 (yǐhòu, "after / from now on"), and a few more time-reference adverbs.
